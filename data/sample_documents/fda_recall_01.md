# Title: FDA recall — Pure Vitamins honey products
Source: https://www.fda.gov/safety/recalls-market-withdrawals-safety-alerts/pure-vitamins-and-natural-supplements-llc-issues-voluntary-nationwide-recall-honey-products-due
Date: 2026-03-14

This starter note is based on an FDA recall notice posted as a company announcement.

Pure Vitamins and Natural Supplements, LLC announced a voluntary nationwide recall of certain honey-style male enhancement products. The FDA summary says the products contained undeclared sildenafil and tadalafil, which are active ingredients used in prescription drugs for erectile dysfunction.

The FDA summary explains that these undeclared ingredients may interact with nitrates found in some prescription medicines and could lower blood pressure to dangerous levels. The affected products were marketed as sexual enhancement honey products and packaged in single-serving plastic tubes.
