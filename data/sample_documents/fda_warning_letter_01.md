# Title: FDA warning letter — Uscom Kft

Source: [https://www.fda.gov/inspections-compliance-enforcement-and-criminal-investigations/warning-letters/uscom-kft-708491-07302025](https://www.fda.gov/inspections-compliance-enforcement-and-criminal-investigations/warning-letters/uscom-kft-708491-07302025)
Date: 2025-07-30

This starter note is based on an FDA warning letter to Uscom Kft concerning medical devices.

According to the FDA letter, an inspection found that the firm manufactured several spirometer products, including SpiroThor and SpiroSonic-branded devices. The letter states that one device, SpiroSonic AIR, was considered adulterated and misbranded because the firm did not have the required approved application or appropriate notification in place.

The warning letter also describes quality-system concerns and says FDA reviewed company respoes and found them inadequate in several areas. The cited issues include corrective and preventive action handling, cleaning and disinfection validation, nonconforming product controls, and complaint or servicing-related procedures.
